Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Ordering

How do I place an order?

To place an order, simply browse our menu, select your items, add them to cart, and proceed to checkout. You'll need to be logged in to complete your order.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, digital wallets (Google Pay, Apple Pay), and cash on delivery in select areas.

Delivery

How long does delivery take?

Delivery times typically range from 20-45 minutes depending on your location and order volume. You can track your order in real-time through our app.

What is the delivery radius?

We deliver within a 5-mile radius of our partner restaurants. Enter your location to see available restaurants in your area.

Account & Support

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button, fill in your details, verify your email address, and you're ready to go!

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team through our Help Center, email at support@foodpal.com, or call us at 1-800-FOODPAL.
